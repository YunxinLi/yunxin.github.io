About Me

A Ph.D. Student from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen. Advised by Prof. Baotian Hu. Long-term cooperation with Dr. Lin Ma, Meituan, Beijing; Prof. Wenhan Luo, Sun Yat-sen University; Yuxiang Wu , University College London.

Past Research Interns: Tencent and advised by Wenhan Luo.

The long-term goal of my research is to help human with more capable artificial intelligence. Dream to building an intelligent metaverse, interesting research directions including:

  • Multimodal Reasoning
  • Human-Machine Interaction
  • Embodied Intelligence
  • Trustworthy AI
